显示连接的WiFi名
netsh
$wifi_name = ( netsh wlan show interface | Where-Object {$_ -match "[^B]SSID*"} ).replace(' ', '').replace('SSID:','')
# .replace('SSID:','WiFi名：')

显示连接的WiFi密码
$wifi_pwd = ( netsh wlan show profile $wifi_name key=clear | Where-Object {$_ -match "关键内容*"} ).replace(' ', '').replace('关键内容:','')
